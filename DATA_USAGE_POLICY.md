# Data Consent and Usage Notice

This dataset contains real facial images and videos collected from volunteers
under informed consent.

All participants agreed that:
- Their data may be used for academic research;
- Their data may be released in anonymized form as a public research dataset;
- The data shall not be used for any commercial purpose.

The dataset is provided strictly for non-commercial academic research.

By downloading or using this dataset, you agree that:
- You will use the data only for academic research;
- You will not attempt to identify any individual in the dataset;
- You will not use the data for surveillance, biometric identification, or any
  other purpose beyond academic research;
- You will not redistribute the data to third parties.

Any use beyond these terms is prohibited.
